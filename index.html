About Us

IPA - Integrated Packaging & Automation delivers automated picking, packaging and palletizing services.

Our Mission

IPA’s mission is to lower the per unit cost of production for its clients. We do this by analyzing the repetitive motion tasks involved in a client’s end-of-line packaging, and developing automation projects that result in enviable return-on-investments for our clients. In order to achieve our goals, we have assembled a staff of highly-experienced experts in the field of robotic technology, custom machine design/build, automated packaging processes, electro-mechanical design, machine vision, software integration and electrical and pneumatic controls. Our staff members have designed, built, and installed palletizer and case packer –related solutions in over 40 states and several countries. We have the most experienced staff not only in Wisconsin but indeed the upper Midwest. We are uniquely structured in the industry with in-house sanitary food-handling design capabilities. With our wide breadth of resources and our focused expertise in end-of-line packaging, our client's needs are served beyond their expectation.

Training

IPA supplies robot technicians certified by the robot manufacturer for the training of end-user personnel. Our training is offered in two formats, Informal and Formal Training.


Informal Training

Informal Training is performed on every cell and includes practical training conducted immediately as the last stage of the start-up process. Informal training includes:

  • Electrical, mechanical, and pneumatic systems
  • System Operation including Programming, Changing Products, End-of Run, Recovery, Screens, Navigation, and Application Parameters
  • System Troubleshooting
  • Preventative Maintenance
  • Safety Training

A printed manual is included beyond the manuals included on-board the robot controller. This manual contains best practices and quick-guides to expedite the learning process, as well as a complete recommended Spare Parts List.


Formal Training

Formal Training is performed optionally and includes both classroom and hands-on training at our production facility. This program is customized to the client’s needs and can incorporate formal verification techniques.
